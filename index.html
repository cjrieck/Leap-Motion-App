About the App

We are developing an application to help solve the infamous Rubik's Cube. The objection is to help people solve the puzzle that still stumps many people today. The Rubik's Cube contains 43 quintillion combinations! A cube that appears to be infinite, yet people continue to solve it under 2 minutes. Our app pairs up with Leap Motion Controller which gives us the ability to simulate the movements of a cube with our hands, so there is a more natural feel. The app also displays SOLUTIONS! an app that can convert any person into a Rubik's whiz! Our Repo! We're open source!
